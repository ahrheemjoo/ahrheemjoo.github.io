Album Cover Designs Song Book for 最後の花弁 (The Meaning of Love) by EGOIST (ft M2U)

About This Project

I selected one of my favorite songs called 最後の花弁 (The Meaning of Love) by EGOIST and M2U to design and produce an 80+ page book dedicated to this one song. I have selected one typeface, Matrix II designed by Zuzana Licko from Emigre, to use throughout the entire book.

Included in the book are 32 album cover designs, stills from my lyric music video exploring the movement of the Matrix II typeface, some interview snippets with EGOIST and M2U, as well as my personal essay that touches on my connection with the song.
