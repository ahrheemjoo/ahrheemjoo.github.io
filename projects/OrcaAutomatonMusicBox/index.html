Orca Automaton Music Box

A mechanical orca that swims gracefully to the melody of a hand-cranked music box

About This Project

An Orca that swims to the music box tune as you turn the winding handle. The orca was designed and cut in Blender and Autodesk Fusion 360. The music box and drawer was designed in Adobe Illustrator, cut by a laser cutter, and hand-assembled through woodworking and sanding.

Creative Process

This project was done using a combination of digital fabrication and traditional woodworking, which brought to life my childhood dream, that is this playful mechanical sculpture!

  • 3D Design: The orca figure was modeled in Blender and Autodesk Fusion 360, to create articulation and balanced weight distribution for the swimming mechanism.
  • Laser Cutting & Assembly: The music box housing and drawer components were designed in Adobe Illustrator and precisely cut using a laser cutter, then carefully sanded and hand-assembled.
  • Mechanical Engineering: The automaton mechanism was designed to translate the rotational motion of the music box handle into the graceful up-and-down swimming motion of the orca.
  • Integration: The music box mechanism and orca automaton were connected to the turning mechanism using gears, resulting in the combination of visual and auditory elements.
