A small obsession

I have a soft spot for vintage things, as they have a lot of history to uncover and sometimes can be a little mysterious (which is fun). Particularly, I'm a music box enthusiast. I have collected over 60 of them, most thrifted, each with its own charm.

There is something magical about a mechanical object that has been singing the same song for decades. I think that is why I keep finding them.

A feathered favorite

Shima
Enaga

I am a big fan of all birds, but if I had to pick one, it would be the Shima Enaga without hesitation. A tiny, round, fluffy little creature native to Hokkaido, Japan. It looks like someone sculpted a snowball and gave it wings.
